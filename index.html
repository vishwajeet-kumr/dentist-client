About the Doctor

Meet Dr. Amrata Srivastava

With over 8 years of dedicated practice in dentistry, Dr. Amrata Srivastava has built a reputation for providing gentle, patient-centered dental care in Jaipur. A graduate in BDS from Kerala, she brings expertise in modern dental techniques combined with a compassionate approach that puts every patient at ease.

Specializing in Dental Implants, Root Canal Treatments, Dentures, and Crown & Bridges, Dr. Amrata is committed to helping you achieve a healthy, confident smile. Her clinic is equipped with the latest dental technology to ensure precise, painless treatments.

Our Dental Services

Root Canal Treatment

Painless root canal procedures using advanced rotary instruments and digital imaging for precise, comfortable treatment.

Dental Implants

Permanent tooth replacement with titanium implants. Natural-looking results that restore your smile and confidence.

Dentures

Custom-fitted full and partial dentures crafted for comfort and a natural appearance. Eat, speak, and smile with ease.

Crown & Bridges

Durable ceramic and zirconia crowns and bridges that blend seamlessly with your natural teeth for a flawless smile.

Teeth Whitening

General Dentistry

Comprehensive dental check-ups, cleanings, fillings, and preventive care for the whole family.
